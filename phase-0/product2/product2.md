    Description
    OptiCalm is a formulation suitable for dogs who suffer from anxiety. It is beneficial for the nervous system and overall well-being of dogs during periods of increased stress.

    There are many signs of stress you should be aware of. Some dogs show it by hyperactive behavior, others hide under the table. If your dog is experiencing longer periods of increased stress it can become depressed or aggressive. 

    Here are some situations that can cause stress, and anxiety to your dog:

    Stormy nights
    Fireworks
    Car rides
    Crowded places
    Guest visits
    Being left at home
    Vet visits

    This product does not compare to sedatives. OptiCalm will make your dog relaxed and a bit sleepy. It will not stop them from greeting your guests at the door but they should be calmer and spend a bit more time sleeping during the day. 

    We recommend you use OptiCalm during stressful situations only.
